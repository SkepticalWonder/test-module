Core Idea

Safety is not a rule sheet. It is a shared habit of noticing risks, communicating clearly, and stopping work when something seems wrong.

Quick Check 1

What is the best first response when you notice an unsafe condition?

Three-Part Safety Habit

  1. Notice: Look for hazards before starting.
  2. Speak: Communicate concerns early.
  3. Act: Stop, fix, or get help when needed.
